On the afternoon of October 31st, the sound of the piano in the East Second Teaching Building was melodious. This is the mid-term music exchange meeting for our school's general elective course "Piano Performance Art".
This mid-term report exchange meeting is one of the teaching links of the course "Piano Performance Art". In the multi-functional hall on the third floor of the East Second Teaching Building, students play a complete piece of music in the form of a grand piano recital, sharing their learning achievements from the first half of the semester with everyone.
